About Myself

I am currently a second year (Sept 2019 - Jun 2020) Ph.D. student in School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), advised by Prof. Dongrui Wu. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Measurement and Control Technology from Wuhan University of Technology in 2018.

My current research interests mainly focus on unsupervised / self-supervised learning, transfer learning and deep representation learning, with their applications to vision and human-machine interface.
